Who Are We?


Psychotic Surfwear is an online apparel store that supplies customers affordable surfwear that they can feel good about. At Psychotic Surfwear we believe that businesses should be accountable for thier enviormental impact and therefore we have implemented the "better than we found it" principle, meaning our products have a positive or neutral impact on the enviorment it comes from. The business will use Zero plastic and eco friendly water based inks to ensure environmental protetion.
