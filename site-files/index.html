Why Shop With Us?


At Psychotic Surfwear, we have made it our #1 priority to ensure that our business practices
meet the highest environmental standards!

Here are just a few examples of changes we've made:


Plastic Free Packaging

No one likes plastic pollution in our oceans - that’s why we don’t use it. We are committed to being entirely plastic-free and make use of both biodegradable labels and mailers when shipping!



Water Based Inks

We ensure that all our products only use Oeko Tex certified water-based inks to help minimise our environmental impact and keep you safe from all those nasty chemicals.



Carbon Offset Manufacturing

Buying online shouldn’t have to cost the planet! All deliveries fulfilled by us are proudly carbon neutral and offset through the direct support of different environmental campaigns.
